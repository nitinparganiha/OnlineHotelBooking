About the ONE 8 Hotel Apartments

One8 Commune is one of the most talked about, hep & happening places in Pune. A venture by the skipper of the Indian Cricket Virat Kohli, this casual dining restaurant is a go-to-place for most sports & food enthusiasts. The restaurant has some of the most amazing looking interiors for it has autographs & Jersey numbers of some of the best cricketers of the World on its walls. The restaurant serves Continental, Mediterranean & Asian cuisine & has gained quite a reputation for selective dishes. The courteous & highly professional staff adds merry to the already wonderful ambiance of this restaurant. Hence, if you are in Pune and want to spend some quality time with friends and family, visit one8 Commune at Rajabahadur City Center, The Mills in Sangamwadi, East Pune.
